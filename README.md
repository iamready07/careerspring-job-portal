# CareerSpring

CareerSpring is a career development and job opportunity platform designed to help students bridge the gap between academics and industry.

The platform enables students to explore career paths, build industry skills, connect with mentors, and apply for internships and jobs.

## Vision

Our mission is simple:

**From Learning to Earning**

CareerSpring aims to become a career ecosystem where students can learn, prepare, and get hired.

## Features

### Job Portal
- Browse internships and full-time jobs
- Search and filter job opportunities
- Apply directly from the platform

### Career Path Guidance
Step-by-step guidance for different careers including:

- Software Development Engineer
- Data Analyst
- Business Analyst
- Product Manager
- UI/UX Designer
- DevOps Engineer
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Cybersecurity Specialist

### Mentorship Platform
Students can connect with industry professionals and get guidance for career growth.

### Student Profiles
Students can create profiles including:

- Skills
- Projects
- Resume
- Certifications

### Company Job Posting
Companies can post job opportunities and hire students.

### Community
Students can discuss career preparation and share opportunities.
